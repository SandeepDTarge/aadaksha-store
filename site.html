Connecting To Your Roots

A Taste of Home, No Matter Where You Roam

Many Maharashtrians travel far from home, settling in other cities across India or crossing oceans. But the heart always yearns for the taste of Aai's (mother's) handmade Puran Poli, the fiery kick of Kolhapuri masala, or the satisfying crunch of Diwali Faral.

Aadaksha was born from this exact emotion—to proudly represent Maharashtrian food in India and bridge the miles for those abroad. We bring you the most authentic, traditionally crafted Maharashtrian delicacies, straight from the rural villages of Maharashtra to your doorstep. Every bite is a memory, every aroma is a journey home.
